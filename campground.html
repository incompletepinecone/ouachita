Campground

Our campgrounds have 14 Primitive Campsites and 4 sites with water and electric hookups for campers and RVs. All of our sites provide privacy for you and your family. There won't be others strolling through the middle of your set up or disturbing your peace. All sites have a fire ring and picnic table. There are campground men's and women's restrooms and showers. Nothin fancy, but there's nice hot water!

Should you forget any important items (toothpaste, first aid, s'more's items, charcoal, etc.), we have many items available for purchase in our gift shop!

River Front Primitive Sites

Many of our primitive sites are RIGHT on the water! Swim and fish right outside your tent, and fall asleep to the sound of the bubbling river.

Electric Sites

The convenience of electricity and running water, combined with the serenity and peace of being in nature. We only book 2 of our 4 sites with electric and water at a time to ensure our campers have space and privacy.

We only accept trailers up to 32 feet

We do not have a septic dumpsite on the property

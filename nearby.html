So what is there to do in the area?

Hiking

So many great hiking trails! Check out this list on AllTrails - Best Nearby Trails

Crystal Mines

We are in the Quartz Crystal capitol of the World! Head to Mt. Ida (a 15 minute drive) and dig up your own crystals to take home at Wegner (with their awesome rock shop and World Famous Phantom Mine) or Twin Creek Crystal Mines!

Fishing

Fish right from our dock! Smallmouth, largemouth, and spotted bass, bluegill, catfish, green and longear sunfish, walleye and other species all call the area home! Get your license here, or use the computers in our office to get one!

Mountain Biking

With 3 local IMBA Epic Trails, we have some awesome riding in our area. The Epic trails nearby are the Womble, Lake Ouachita Vista Trail, and Ouachita Trail. There are many other trails in the National Forest as well! Let us help you get from point A to point B with a mountain bike shuttle!.
